Bio

I'm a currently enrolled Ph.D. student at the SLD group at the University of Texas at Austin under the supervision of Dr. Radu Marculescu. I obtained my Bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in June 2018. My current research interests include:

  • Trustworthy ML and Generative AI: Enhancing the trustworthiness of machine learning by exerting control over the training data and generated content in Generative AI.
  • Efficient Deep Learning System Design: Accelerating both training and inference efficiency of large-scale models.
