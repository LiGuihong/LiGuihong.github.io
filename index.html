Bio

I'm a MTS (Member of Technical Staff) at AMD where I focus on efficient AI system design. Before joining AMD, I obtained my Ph.D. degree from UT Austin in May 2024 and Bachelor's degree from Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT) in June 2018.
My current research interests include:

  • Efficient Generative AI Models design: I am doing the research to build the next-gen of model achitecture to accelerate inference throughput, including foundation LLMs and diffusion models.
  • Efficient Training Systems: I am building efficient, reliable and large-scale data centers to enable the training of large foundation AI models. My work focuses on improving training performance on Megatron-LM, JAX, and native PyTorch on AMD hardware platforms. This involves deep system profiling to locate bottlenecks and applying targeted optimizations to resolve them.
